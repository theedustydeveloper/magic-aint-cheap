Core Mechanics

Economy

Gold System

Everything costs Gold. Every spell, weapon, armor piece, and enhancement must be purchased. Manage your resources carefully‚Äîrunning out of Gold means running out of options.

Combat

Dice Pool System

Your dice pool represents your energy and actions. Start with one die and earn more by defeating Bosses. Use dice to attack, cast spells, and activate abilities. Exhausted dice return after completing challenges.

Strategy

The Green Room

Between challenges, rest in the Green Room‚Äîa safe zone where you can heal through Resting Rolls, shop for better gear, and prepare for what's ahead. Time is money, and every decision counts.
