Welcome to The Gauntlet

You've been selected to compete in The Gauntlet, the galaxy's most popular death sport and the ultimate test of skill, strategy, and survival. Broadcast across countless systems, this dystopian game show throws contestants into deadly challenges where magic isn't just power—it's currency.

Every spell you cast, every weapon you swing, every piece of armor you wear must be purchased with hard-earned Gold. The cameras are rolling, the audience is watching, and only the strongest, smartest, and wealthiest will survive.

Game Overview

Player Count & Cooperation

Magic Ain't Cheap is designed for 1-4 players working together as a team. This is a fully cooperative game—you win together or fail together. The game automatically scales difficulty based on your party size.

Difficulty Modes

  • Easy Mode: Draw 6 cards during Reward Drafts. Trade freely with teammates.
  • Medium Mode: Draw 5 cards during Reward Drafts. Trading costs 5 Gold per card.
  • Hard Mode: Draw only 4 cards during Reward Drafts. Trading forbidden.

Game Length

A complete campaign takes approximately 2-3 hours to complete all three Floors:

  • Floor 1: 30-45 minutes
  • Floor 2: 45-60 minutes
  • Floor 3: 60-90 minutes

The Gauntlet Structure

The Gauntlet consists of three brutal Floors, each with six deadly Challenges and one massive Boss fight. You'll start with almost nothing—just one die and a handful of basic equipment. As you progress, you'll level up, gain more dice, and unlock increasingly powerful gear.

Core Mechanics

Gold Economy

Everything costs Gold. Every spell, weapon, armor piece, and enhancement must be purchased. Manage your resources carefully—running out of Gold means running out of options.

Dice Pool System

Your dice pool represents your energy and actions. Start with one die and earn more by defeating Bosses. Use dice to attack, cast spells, and activate abilities. Exhausted dice return after completing challenges.

The Green Room

Between challenges, rest in the Green Room—a safe zone where you can heal through Resting Rolls, shop for better gear, and prepare for what's ahead. Time is money, and every decision counts.

Death & Revival

Death is temporary in The Gauntlet. Thanks to corporate sponsorship and cutting-edge revival technology, contestants respawn in the Green Room after being knocked out. However, failure comes with consequences: you'll lose precious Gold, take heavy damage, and fall behind your competitors.

Win Condition

Can you and your team survive all three Floors and defeat the Final Boss? Or will you run out of Gold, HP, or luck before the final credits roll?

Let's find out. The show is about to begin.
