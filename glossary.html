GLOSSARY

Quick reference for all game terms and mechanics in Magic Ain't Cheap.

STAT TAGS - SPECIAL MECHANICS

Stat Tags Overview

Stat Tags are special gameplay tags on powerful cards. Cards with Stat Tags can only be used ONCE per turn and exhaust when used. At end of round, they reset if you have +3 or higher in the required stat. Otherwise they stay exhausted until start of your next turn.

Heavy Tag (Might): Raw power weapons/armor requiring +3 Might to reset. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Heavy Armor, Big Shield, "Big Hit" enhancement.
Finesse Tag (Speed): Agile abilities/light weapons requiring +3 Speed to reset. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: "On The Button", "Dodge", "Cheap Shot".
Arcane Tag (Intelligence): Magical items/abilities requiring +3 INT to reset. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Wands, Arcane Robes, "Quick Hands", "Brainstorm".
Sacred Tag (Willpower): Holy spells/spiritual abilities requiring +3 Willpower to reset. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Healing spells, "Quick Wits", "Mind Power".
Fancy Tag (Charm): Stylish weapons/fashionable armor requiring +3 Charm to reset. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Fine Leather sets, RBF Knife, "Mocking Words", performance abilities.
Risky Tag (Luck): Gambling abilities/lucky items requiring +3 Luck to reset. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: "Slay for Pay", fortune-based effects.
Supernatural Tag (INT + Willpower): SPECIAL - Requires BOTH +3 INT AND +3 Willpower to reset. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Blood Magic, Necromancy, Invisibility. Most demanding stat tag.
Strategy Tip:

Invest in getting one stat to +3 early to unlock reliable use of Stat Tag cards. Without +3, these powerful cards stay exhausted between rounds, limiting their effectiveness!

A

Ability: A card type that provides special effects, passive or active.
Accessory Slot: Equipment slot for rings, amulets, trinkets (1 per player).
AOE (Area of Effect): Attacks or effects that hit multiple targets.
Arcane Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Intelligence to reset. Magical weapons, robes, spell-boosting abilities. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Wands, Arcane Robes, Magic Staff, "Quick Hands", "Brainstorm".
Arms Slot: Equipment slot for arm guards, bracers, sleeves (1 per player).
Armor: Cards that provide Block, equipped in specific body slots.
Attack: An action where you roll a die to deal damage to a target.

B

Base Damage: The damage listed on a weapon/spell card before modifiers.
Berserker: Keyword ability - when deals damage, immediately attack another random target.
Block: Armor value that reduces incoming damage (cannot reduce below 1).
Body Slot: Equipment slot for chest armor, robes, jackets (1 per player).
Boss: A special super-powered monster at the end of each Floor.

C

Challenge: An encounter the party must complete (Combat, Skill, or Hybrid).
Charm (Ch): A stat used for social abilities and Clone Token scaling.
Clone Token: A Charm-based summoned ally.
Critical Hit: Rolling natural max on die = double damage.
Co-op: Cooperative - players work together to win.

D

Damage: Reduction of HP caused by attacks.
Death: Reaching 0 HP - player is knocked out, respawns after Challenge.
Dice Affinity: Recommended die to use with a card (listed on card).
Dice Pool: The collection of dice a player owns (max 6).
Difficulty Mode: Easy/Medium/Hard - chosen at game start, affects draft count and trading.
Discard Pile: Where used/discarded cards go (communal, can buy from here).
Dual Wield: Equipping two weapons (one in each Hand slot).

E

Easy Mode: Reward Draft difficulty - draw 6 cards, free trading.
Enhancements: Card type that permanently modifies stats (90 in Floor 1). Never go in inventory - attach to character forever.
Environment: A card that affects all participants in a Challenge.
Equipment Slot: Body location where gear is equipped (Head/Body/Arms/Legs/Hands/Accessory).
Evasive: Keyword ability - gain +1 Block per Miss (resets each Round).
Exhaust: Rotating a card sideways - cannot use again this Round.
Exhausted Dice Pool: Area of player board where used dice go. Dice reset at end of turn/round.

F

Failure: Not completing a Challenge's Win Condition (or time limit reached).
Fancy Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Charm to reset. Stylish weapons, fashionable armor, performance abilities. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Fine Leather sets, RBF Knife, Brass Knuckles, "Mocking Words".
Favors: Card type with one-time powerful effects from production/audience (18 in Floor 1). Can be used in Green Room or Challenge. Stay in inventory until used.
Finesse Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Speed to reset. Agile abilities, light weapons requiring precision. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: "On The Button", "Dodge", "Cheap Shot".
Floor: A set of 6 Challenges + 1 Boss (game has 3 Floors).
Free Action: Action that doesn't cost resources (communicate, check stats, etc.).

G

Gold (G): Currency used to buy cards.
Green Room: Safe zone between Challenges where players rest, shop, and prepare.
Guardian Token: A Willpower-based summoned ally.

H

Hands Slots: Equipment slots for weapons, shields, gloves (2 per player).
Hard Mode: Reward Draft difficulty - draw 4 cards, no trading.
Head Slot: Equipment slot for helmets, hats, hoods (1 per player).
Heavy Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Might to reset. Raw power weapons, brutal armor, strength-based abilities. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Heavy Armor, Big Shield, "Big Hit" enhancement.
High Test: Skill Test where you ADD stat to roll (trying to roll high).
HP (Hit Points): Health - when reduced to 0, player/monster dies.
Hybrid Challenge: A Challenge with both combat and skill test elements.

I

Initiative: Turn order value (roll die + Speed stat for players).
Intelligence (INT): A stat used for spells and INT Tests.
Inventory: Owned cards that are not currently equipped.
Item: A card type that provides passive effects.

K

Keyword Ability: A special monster ability (Berserker, Pack Hunter, Evasive, etc.).
Kits: Card type - large equipment usable only in Green Room (15 in Floor 1). Requires die sacrifice (move to exhaust without rolling). Provides buff until end of next Challenge.

L

Legs Slot: Equipment slot for leg armor, pants, greaves (1 per player).
Level: Player power tier (1-6), determines available dice count.
Level Up: Gaining +1 die after completing 3 Challenges or defeating a Boss.
Low Test: Skill Test where you SUBTRACT stat from roll (trying to roll low).
Luck (L): A stat used for Luck Tests and some abilities.

M

Main Action: An action that requires a resource (die, HP, Gold, etc.).
Medium Mode: Reward Draft difficulty - draw 5 cards, 5G to trade.
Might (M): A stat used for weapon attacks.
Miss: Rolling natural 1 on die = attack fails (no damage).
Monster: An enemy creature controlled by the game.

P

Pack Hunter: Keyword ability - +2 damage per allied monster.
Phase: A section of Boss behavior based on HP thresholds.
Poison: Keyword ability - on hit, target takes 2 HP/turn for 3 rounds.

R

Reaction: An ability that triggers in response to specific events.
Recall: Voluntarily exiting a Challenge (counts as Failure).
Regeneration: Keyword ability - heal X HP at start of turn.
Resting Roll: Rolling one of your dice to heal HP in Green Room (FREE, no cost).
Reward Deck: The 600-card deck containing all purchasable cards.
Reward Draft: Drafting cards after a Challenge (Easy/Medium/Hard modes).
Risky Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Luck to reset. Gambling abilities, fortune-based items, high-stakes maneuvers. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: "Slay for Pay", lucky items.
Round: A full cycle where every participant takes 1 turn.

S

Skeleton Token: An INT-based summoned ally.
Skill Challenge: A Challenge requiring Stat Tests (non-combat).
Skill Test: Rolling a die + modding with a stat to achieve High or Low result.
Sacred Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Willpower to reset. Holy spells, divine abilities, spiritual techniques. Once per turn, exhausts on use. Examples: Healing spells, "Quick Wits", "Mind Power".
Speed (S): A stat used for Initiative, Speed Tests, and some weapons.
Spell: A magic-based attack card that scales with INT.
Stat: One of 6 attributes (Might, Speed, INT, Willpower, Charm, Luck).
Stat Tag: Special gameplay tags (Heavy, Finesse, Arcane, Sacred, Fancy, Risky, Supernatural) on powerful cards. Cards with Stat Tags can only be used once per turn and exhaust when used. Reset at end of round if you have +3 or higher in the required stat. See detailed Stat Tags section above for full mechanics and strategy.
Supernatural Tag: SPECIAL Stat Tag requiring BOTH +3 Intelligence AND +3 Willpower to reset. Most demanding stat tag. Powerful spells like Blood Magic, Necromancy, Invisibility. Once per turn, exhausts on use.
Success: Completing a Challenge's Win Condition.

T

Time Limit: Maximum number of Rounds before a Challenge auto-fails.
Token: A summoned ally creature (Skeleton, Clone, or Guardian).
Treasure: Bonus loot randomly available in some Challenges.
Treasure Guardian: Keyword ability - auto-succeed Treasure Roll on defeat.
Treasure Roll: Rolling at Challenge start to determine if Treasure appears.
Turn: One participant's opportunity to take actions.

U

Unactivated Dice Pool: Area of player board where available/unused dice are kept. Dice move to Exhausted Pool when used.

W

Weapon: A physical attack card that scales with Might or Speed.
Willpower (WP): A stat used for resisting effects and Guardian Token scaling.
Win Condition: What must be achieved to succeed at a Challenge.
