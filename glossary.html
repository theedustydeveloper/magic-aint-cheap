GLOSSARY

Quick reference for all game terms and mechanics in Magic Ain't Cheap. Your essential guide to understanding The Gauntlet.

A

Ability: A card type that provides special effects, passive or active.
Accessory Slot: Equipment slot for rings, amulets, trinkets (1 per player).
Aggro X: Force X Monsters or Players to change their attack target to you.
AOE (Area of Effect): Attacks or effects that hit multiple targets.
Arcane Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Intelligence to reset. Found on magical weapons, robes, and spell-boosting abilities. Exhausts on use; resets at end of round if INT is +3 or higher.
Arms Slot: Equipment slot for arm guards, bracers, sleeves (1 per player).
Armor: Cards that provide Block, equipped in specific body slots.
Attack: An action where you roll a die to deal damage to a target.

B

Base Damage: The damage listed on a weapon/spell card before modifiers.
Berserker: When this deals damage, immediately attack another random target.
Block: Armor value that reduces incoming damage (cannot reduce below 1).
Body Slot: Equipment slot for chest armor, robes, jackets (1 per player).
Boss: A special super-powered monster at the end of each Floor.
Burst: Spells with Burst allow the player to commit any number of available dice when casting. All dice used this way are exhausted and impact an attribute of the spell.

C

Challenge: An encounter the party must complete (Combat, Skill, or Hybrid).
Channel X: To activate this spell, the player must spend X turns placing one die on the card per turn. When the player starts their turn with X dice on the card, they may activate the spell without exhausting a die from the Active Dice Pool. The player takes -2 Attack Damage for each die currently on the card.
Charm (Ch): A stat used for social abilities and Clone Token scaling.
Clone Token: A Charm-based summoned ally. Counters = Ch. Stats: 1 HP, 1 Block, 1 + X DMG.
Cooldown X: After using this card, it cannot be used again for X Rounds.
Critical Hit: Rolling natural max on die = double damage.

D

Damage: Reduction of HP caused by attacks.
Death: Reaching 0 HP - player is knocked out, respawns after Challenge.
Demoralize X: X target Monsters' next attack deals -3 damage.
Dice Affinity: Recommended die to use with a card. When a card references a die bonus, it appears as [DX] (e.g., [D8], [D20]).
Dice Pool: The collection of dice a player owns (max 6).
Discard: Permanently remove a card from your hand or inventory to activate its effect. Discarded cards go to the Discard Pile.
Discard Pile: Where used/discarded cards go (communal, can buy from here).
Distracted X: Target has -X Nudge, -X Block,and -X DMG until the end of their next turn.
Dual Wield: Equipping two weapons (one in each Hand slot).

E

Enhancements: Card type that permanently modifies stats. Never go in inventory - attach to character forever.
Environment: A card that affects all participants in a Challenge.
Equipment Slot: Body location where gear is equipped (Head/Body/Arms/Legs/Hands/Accessory).
Evasive: Gain +1 Block per Miss (resets each Round).
Exhaust: Rotate a card sideways to activate its effect — it cannot be used again until reset.
Exhausted Dice Pool: Area of player board where used dice go. Dice reset at end of turn/round.

F

Failure: Not completing a Challenge's Win Condition (or time limit reached).
Fancy Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Charm to reset. Found on stylish weapons, fashionable armor, and performance abilities. Exhausts on use; resets at end of round if Charm is +3 or higher.
Favors: Card type with one-time powerful effects. Stay in inventory until used.
Finesse Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Speed to reset. Found on agile abilities and light weapons requiring precision. Exhausts on use; resets at end of round if Speed is +3 or higher.
Floor: A set of 6 Challenges + 1 Boss (game has 3 Floors).
Free Action: Action that doesn't cost resources (communicate, check stats, etc.).
Fumble: Rolling a natural 1 on an attack die. The attack fails and may trigger an additional negative effect.

G

Gold (G): Currency used to buy cards.
Green Room: Safe zone between Challenges where players rest, shop, and prepare.
Guard +X: Spend your Action to gain +X additional Block until the start of your next turn.
Guardian Token: A Willpower-based summoned ally. Counters = WP. Stats: 1 HP, 1 + X Block, 1 DMG.

H

Hands Slots: Equipment slots for weapons, shields, gloves (2 per player).
Head Slot: Equipment slot for helmets, hats, hoods (1 per player).
Heavy Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Might to reset. Found on raw power weapons, brutal armor, and strength-based abilities. Exhausts on use; resets at end of round if Might is +3 or higher.
High Test: Skill Test where you ADD stat to roll (trying to roll high).
HP (Hit Points): Health - when reduced to 0, player/monster dies.
Hybrid Challenge: A Challenge with both combat and skill test elements.

I

Initiative: Turn order value (roll die + Speed stat for players).
Intelligence (INT): A stat used for spells and INT Tests.
Inventory: Owned cards that are not currently equipped.
Item: A card type that provides passive effects.

K

Keyword Ability: A special ability found on Monster cards (Berserker, Pack Hunter, Evasive, etc.).
Kits: Card type - large equipment usable only in Green Room. Requires die sacrifice. Provides buff until end of next Challenge.

L

Legs Slot: Equipment slot for leg armor, pants, greaves (1 per player).
Level: Player power tier (1-6), determines available dice count.
Level Up: Gaining +1 die after completing 3 Challenges or defeating a Boss.
Low Test: Skill Test where you SUBTRACT stat from roll (trying to roll low).
Luck (L): A stat used for Luck Tests and some abilities.

M

Main Action: An action that requires a resource (die, HP, Gold, etc.).
Might (M): A stat used for weapon attacks.
Miss: Rolling natural 1 on die = attack fails (no damage).
Monster: An enemy creature controlled by the game.

P

Pack Hunter: +2 damage per allied monster in play.
Phase: A section of Boss behavior based on HP thresholds.
Poison: On hit, target takes 2 HP/turn for 3 rounds.
Prep: Found on Kit cards. The listed buff is applied at the start of the next Challenge.
Prone X: Target cannot make any actions until it exhausts X Dice to remove the status effect.

R

Reaction: An ability that triggers in response to specific events.
Recall: Voluntarily exiting a Challenge (counts as Failure).
Regeneration: Heal X HP at the start of each turn.
Resting Roll: Rolling one of your dice to heal HP in Green Room (FREE, no cost).
Reward Deck: The 600-card deck containing all purchasable cards.
Reward Draft: Drafting cards after a Challenge (Easy/Medium/Hard modes).
Risky Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Luck to reset. Found on gambling abilities, fortune-based items, and high-stakes maneuvers. Exhausts on use; resets at end of round if Luck is +3 or higher.
Round: A full cycle where every participant takes 1 turn.

S

Sacred Tag: Stat Tag requiring +3 Willpower to reset. Found on holy spells, divine abilities, and spiritual techniques. Exhausts on use; resets at end of round if Willpower is +3 or higher.
Skill Challenge: A Challenge requiring Stat Tests (non-combat).
Skeleton Token: An INT-based summoned ally. Counters = INT. Stats: 1 + X HP, 1 Block, 1 DMG.
Skill Test: Rolling a die + modding with a stat to achieve High or Low result.
Speed (S): A stat used for Initiative, Speed Tests, and some weapons.
Spell: A magic-based attack card that scales with INT.
Stat: One of 6 attributes (Might, Speed, INT, Willpower, Charm, Luck).
Stat Tag: A special property on powerful cards. Cards with Stat Tags exhaust when used and can only be used once per turn. Reset at end of round if required stat is met.
Supernatural Tag: Special Stat Tag requiring both +3 Intelligence AND +3 Willpower to reset. Found on the most powerful spells. Exhausts on use.
Success: Completing a Challenge's Win Condition.

T

Time Limit: Maximum number of Rounds before a Challenge auto-fails.
Token: A summoned ally creature. Scaling based on Counters (X):
  • • Clone (Ch): 1 HP, 1 Block, 1 + X DMG
  • • Guardian (WP): 1 HP, 1 + X Block, 1 DMG
  • • Skeleton (INT): 1 + X HP, 1 Block, 1 DMG
Treasure: Bonus loot randomly available in some Challenges.
Treasure Guardian: Auto-succeed Treasure Roll on defeat.
Treasure Roll: Rolling at Challenge start to determine if Treasure appears.
Turn: One participant's opportunity to take actions.

U

Unactivated Dice Pool: Area of player board where available/unused dice are kept.

W

Weapon: A physical attack card that scales with Might or Speed.
Willpower (WP): A stat used for resisting effects and Guardian Token scaling.
Win Condition: What must be achieved to succeed at a Challenge.
