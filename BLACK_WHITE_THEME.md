# BLACK & WHITE THEME - Sanitized Design

## Overview
The website has been completely redesigned with a clean, professional black and white aesthetic. All ArcanePunk styling (purple, orange, gradients, neon effects) has been removed in favor of a minimalist, high-contrast design.

### **Colors & Gradients**

- Purple color scheme (#8b4be3, #4a1e7a, #c77dff)
- Orange color scheme (#ff6b35, #d94e1f, #ffaa00)
- Neon cyan accents (#00ffff)
- Metal/grease/leather themed colors
- All gradient backgrounds
- All box shadows
- All text shadows
- Colored borders and glows

### **Complex Styling**
‚ùå Removed:
- Gradient navigation backgrounds
- Neon border effects

### **Simplified Design Elements**

**Navigation:**
- Pure black background
- White text and logo (inverted via CSS filter)
- Simple gray hover states
- Clean border separators
- Minimalist hamburger menu

**Content:**
- White backgrounds with subtle gray borders
- Black text throughout
- Light gray (#f8f9fa) for secondary backgrounds
- High contrast for readability

**Tables:**
- Black headers with white text
- White body with light gray row hover
- Simple 1px borders
- Clean, professional appearance

**Card Modals:**
- White cards with black borders
- Simple 4px black top accent bar
- Gray backgrounds for stat sections
- Black and dark gray badges
- No shadows or glows

**Buttons:**
- Black background, white text
- Inverts on hover (white bg, black text)
- Simple 2px borders
- No gradients or complex effects

**Floor Generator:**
- Hidden challenges use pure black background
- Revealed challenges use light gray
- Simple black borders
- Clean, minimal design

## Design Philosophy

**High Contrast:**
- Black on white for maximum readability
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Professional appearance

**Minimal Decoration:**
- Only essential styling
- No decorative gradients or shadows
- Focus on content over aesthetics

**Clean Borders:**
- Simple 1px and 2px borders
- No colored or glowing effects
- Straight lines and sharp corners

**Professional Typography:**
- System font stack for performance
- Clear hierarchy with font sizes and weights
- Proper spacing and line height
